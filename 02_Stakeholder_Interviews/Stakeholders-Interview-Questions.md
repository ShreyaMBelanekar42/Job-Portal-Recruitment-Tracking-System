# STAKEHOLDER INTERVIEW QUESTIONS - INITIAL DRAFT

### Questions for HR:
1. What tools do you currently use to track shortlisted candidates (Excel, email, ATS, etc.), and what challenges do you face with accuracy and version control?
How many applications do you receive per role on a daily basis?
2. Generally, how much time do you need to go through one application manually?
3. What issues do you face in the current Applications shortlisting process?
4. How long does shortlisting take today?
5. Can you estimate how many qualified candidates are lost monthly due to the current manual process?
6. How hard is it to maintain the data of shortlisted candidates in the current system?

### Questions for Hiring Manager:
1. What is the current procedure followed after shortlisting the applications?
2. Where/How do you maintain the resumes of shortlisted applications?
3. How do you update the candidates about hiring positions like whether they are shortlisted/rejected/hired?
4. How does the current interview scheduling process work?
5. How much time does it take to schedule an interview?

### Questions for Candidates:
1. How much time does it take to actually send application emails and hear back from recruiters?
2. Would you like to have a system where you can apply to jobs, check status and have interviews in one place, will it be beneficial?

### Business Objective questions for HR:
1. As a HR, would you like to see total applications received, shortlisted candidates count, interviews scheduled on dashboard?
2. Who are all users that will be using this system?
3. Do you want the filters to see the candidates list for (shortlisted/rejected/not viewed/viewed)?
4. Do you want any option to share the candidate profiles with Hiring Managers?
5. What features Hiring Managers can have access to?
6. If you want to receive any notification, how do you want it, in-app, message or email?
7. What important features do you want in Phase 1?
8. What is the target deadline for system launch?

### Questions for Security, Compliance & Backup:
1. Do you want candidate data to be encrypted?
2. In case of Internet Failure, any backup?

# STAKEHOLDER INTERVIEW QUESTIONS - FINAL DRAFT

### Questions for HR:
1. Can you walk me through the current application shortlisting process from receiving resumes to final selection?
2. On average, how many applications do you receive daily/weekly?
3. How much time does it take to review one application manually?
4. What are the major issues you face in the current manual shortlisting process?
5. How often do you feel that good candidates are missed due to manual handling?
6. How difficult is it to store, search, and track shortlisted candidate data currently?
7. Would you like automatic resume screening based on skills, experience, location, and salary?
8. What should happen when a resume matches the job criteria – auto-shortlist or manual approval?
9. Do you want a resume parsing feature (extract name, skills, education automatically)?
10. As an HR, would you like a dashboard showing:
    * Total applications received
    * Shortlisted candidates
    * Interviews scheduled
    * Candidates selected/rejected
11. Do you want monthly hiring reports and recruiter performance reports?
12. How would you like to receive notifications – in-app, email, or SMS?
13. Who all from your organization will use this system?
14. Should different users have different access rights (HR, Recruiter, Hiring Manager)?

### Questions for Hiring Manager:
1. What is the current process after applications are shortlisted?
2. Where and how do you maintain shortlisted candidate resumes today?
3. How do you currently update candidates about selection/rejection/hiring?
4. How does the current interview scheduling process work?
5. How much time usually goes from shortlisting to actual interview completion?
6. Do you want a scorecard system for interview evaluation?
7. Should interview feedback be mandatory before moving to the next round?
8. Would you like auto-generated offer approval workflows?

### Questions for Recruiters:
1. How do you currently track candidate status (new, reviewed, shortlisted, interview, selected, rejected)?
2. Do you want the system to auto-update candidate status after each stage?
3. Do you want bulk resume uploads and bulk candidate rejection emails?
4. Would you like to schedule interviews directly from the system?
5. Do you want Google Calendar / Outlook integration for interviews?

### Questions for Candidates:
1. Currently, how much time does it take to apply and get a response from recruiters?
2. Would you like a single system where you can apply for jobs, track status, and attend interviews?
3. How would you prefer to attend interviews:
    * Video interview
    * Telephonic
    * In-person
4. Would you like real-time notifications for:
    * Application viewed
    * Shortlisted
    * Interview scheduled
    * Selected / Rejected
5. Would you prefer a mobile app, web app, or both?
6. How long would you like to access your job application history (6 months / 1 year / lifetime)?

### Questions for Security, Compliance & Backup:
1. Do you want candidate data to be encrypted?
2. Are there any legal compliance requirements like GDPR or data retention policies?
3. In case of internet/system failure, do you want:
    * Manual fallback
    * Auto data sync later
    * What devices will users mostly use:
    * Mobile
    * Laptop
    * Desktop

### Questions for Business objectives and phase wise deliverables
1. What are the most important goals of this system?
    * Faster hiring
    * Reduced workload
    * Better quality candidates
2. What features must be completed in Phase 1 (MVP)?
3. What is the target deadline for system launch?
4. What is the expected reduction in hiring cycle time after system implementation?
